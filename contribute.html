Contribution Guidelines

Wiki

The Wiki is open to all registered users. We are looking for tutorials, guides, and any information on component/hardware measurements and behavior that is relevant to batteryless/intermittent devices. More information on documentation guidelines/best practices can be found on the Wiki.

Artifacts

The Artifacts Directory is a curated resource for hardware and software designs, applications and energy trace data related to the design and development of intermittent and batteryless devices for reference and use by the community. Primary considerations for acceptance to the directory are:
  • Completeness: Is the artifact complete to a functional level?
  • Documentation: Is sufficient documentation available to use or implement the artifact?
  • Functionality/Accuracy: does the artifact work as intended and/or provide valid information?
Contact us if you have an artifact that you would like evaluated for inclusion into the directory. Support is available to help refine artifacts for inclusion as needed.
